Electronic Press Kit

The Purpose of this website is to showcase my music for fans and any potential collaborators.

The Audience of this website is intended for fans, professional booking agents, and/or news reporters/journalists. To boil it down, it's for people interested in my music. Anyone who wants to listen to my music, anyone who wants to make money with me on my music, or anyone who wants to promote and/or write about my music.

The Goal of this project is to:

  1. Attract music fans, promoters, journalists, booking agents, and venue owners.
  2. Serve as a showcase for my music, where I've played, where I've been published, and my contact information
  3. Sell albums, singles, merchandise (eventually)
  4. Act as an official source of updated information regarding my music

Persona

Name: Gavin

Age: 26

Profession: Founder of a songwriters collective looking for new talent and bringing the community together. They also have a main job they report to and the songwriter's collective is more of a passion project. They work odd jobs, and currently they're working at a bar as a bartender.

Technology: Macbook user, Monterey OS, safari, and has an iPhone.

Attitudes and Behaviors: Doesn't have much time to waste as he has a family, work, and his passion project of bringing the music community together. He doesn't want to waste time on people that are not very serious about what they're doing and only wants to find the best to join the collective. The events and concerts he organizes are open to the public, but he only wants top talent to attract people and get them excited to go to music events around town.

Frustrations and Needs: He has a short temper with people who act like they are serious about music, but are only in it for ego, money, or status. He needs to find top talent, and he does not care where. He's also frustrated with how unprofessional a lot of talented musicians can be whether it be showing up late to an event, not respecting his time, or not having an electronic press kit.

Goals: His main goal is to birth a lively and competitive music scene that brings the community together and produces top-level and professional musicians. He wants to create a new kind of nashville, but not the nashville that currently exists. His only concern is: will this musician respect my time and others by being professional and humble? He's not looking for braggadocios individuals as those people are usually not in it for the music and take they're position for granted whatever it might be.

User Story: As a user, I need to determine whether this musician's electronic press kit website reflects well on them as an individual.

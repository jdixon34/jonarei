Biography

I started playing guitar as a freshman in high school for a girl, go figure. After that I fell in love with music as a whole and began my music journey by listening to classic rock hits of the 70's to hip hop/rap in the early 2,000's. I developed a deep love of music over time as I listened to different varieties and genres of music. I had grown up in an amazing family and a good household, so I was very shielded from the horrors of life.

So when a friend of mine committed suicide in my senior year of high school, I didn't know how to deal with it. The only way I knew how was through music as I was also very silent about my own problems and feelings. I didn't open up until I started writing songs about how I felt and my experiences through that tough time so through that I found an outlet. However, as I got into college I fell into a deep depression after losing a family member. I felt so unmotivated to do anything else other than music and failed my classes two semesters in a row. I came home and realized that this depression I was going through had the potential to end my life by my own means, so I did a lot of thinking and introspection.

I realized the only way I could find a will to live was through my love of music. Ever since August 2017, I've decided that I want to take music a lot more seriously and to try to make it in the business. I'm a Native American so my chances are slim, but that doesn't deter me from trying.
